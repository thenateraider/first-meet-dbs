## 1. Describe the human problems
* It is hard for homeowners and small businesses to find trusted local technicians for quick or urgent repairs
* Customers worry about safety when technicians get their private phone numbers or emails directly
* Traditional repair services have hidden fees, unfair prices, and no warranties
* Good technicians struggle to find local customers and lack secure digital payment tools

## 2. Describe how your solution solves these problems
* We sell repair services as simple tickets with fixed prices and clear work details
* Customers order a service and the system automatically assigns the closest verified technician
* We protect customer privacy by blocking direct contact and using a secure chat channel inside the website
* We hold the customer's payment in a secure system and only pay the technician after the work is approved
* We offer single payments for quick jobs and step-by-step progress payments for big projects

## 3. Describe your ecommerce business idea including core features
**Business Idea**
ChangHub.io is an online store that sells fixed-price repair tickets and matches customers with trusted local technicians while protecting user privacy.

**Core Features**
* **Browse Services** - Customers can view categorized service vouchers like AC maintenance, electrical repair, plumbing, and locksmith services, or request custom quotes for large-scale projects.
* **Add to Cart** - Customers can select the services they want and set quantities, such as booking 3 air conditioner cleaning vouchers, and add them to the shopping cart.
* **Place Order and Auto-Assignment** - The customer enters the service address and describes the problem. The system then automatically assigns the nearest vetted technician.
* **Escrow Payment** - Customers pay securely online through credit cards or QR codes. The platform holds this money in a safe escrow system.
* **Secure In-App Chat** - A private chat channel opens inside the website to connect the customer and technician. This keeps private phone numbers and emails completely hidden.
* **Approve and Payout**
  * **Pay-per-Job** - The customer reviews the finished job and clicks to approve, releasing the full escrowed money to the technician all at once.
  * **Milestone Payments** - For big projects like full house rewiring, the work is split into steps. The customer reviews and approves each step individually to release the money progressively.
* **Admin Dispatch Dashboard** - Administrators can manage the service catalog, track active orders, and help resolve customer issues.
