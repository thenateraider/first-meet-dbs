## 1. Describe the human problems
* **Finding Trusted Help is Difficult:** It is hard for homeowners and small businesses to find trusted, verified local technicians for quick repairs or specialized projects.
* **Privacy and Safety Concerns:** Customers worry about personal privacy and safety when technicians obtain their private phone numbers or emails directly.
* **Lack of Transparency:** Traditional repair services often involve hidden fees, unfair pricing, and lack clear service agreements.
* **Technician Business Limitations:** Skilled technicians struggle to find local customers, lack marketing channels, and don't have secure digital tools to handle custom quotations or milestone payments.

## 2. Describe how your solution solves these problems
* **Verified Open Marketplace:** We provide an online marketplace where verified technicians can list their fixed-price service presets or offer custom tailored services.
* **Flexible Hiring System:** We offer a flexible hiring system: customers can instantly purchase preset tickets for simple jobs or request custom quotes for large-scale projects.
* **Complete Privacy Masking:** We protect user privacy by completely masking private contact details, routing all communication through a secure, in-app chat channel.
* **Secure Slip-Verified Escrow:** We feature a secure escrow payment system via bank transfer and slip verification, holding the funds safely until the customer approves the completed work.
* **Adaptable Payment Structures:** We support multiple payment models, including single payments for quick jobs and progressive milestone payments for large, multi-stage projects.

## 3. Describe your ecommerce business idea including core features

**Business Idea**
ChangHub.io is an online marketplace for home and technical services that sells fixed-price repair tickets (Presets) and provides customized service agreements. The platform matches customers with trusted, verified local technicians while protecting user privacy and ensuring fair payments through an escrow system.

**Core Features**
* **Technician Onboarding & Verification**
  * **Application & Categorization:** Technicians register, submit credentials, and specify their areas of expertise (e.g., Air Conditioning, Electrical, Plumbing).
  * **Admin Approval:** Admins review and approve technician profiles before they can list services on the platform to ensure quality and safety.
* **Browse & Discovery**
  * Customers can easily browse and filter services by category. They can view various preset service options created by verified technicians, along with pricing, ratings, and technician profiles.
* **Chat and Agreement (Flexible Hiring Modes)**
  Once a customer selects a technician, the hiring process can happen in two ways:
  * **Preset Order:** The customer buys a pre-defined service at a fixed price paid in a single transaction.
  * **Custom Scope Agreement:** For jobs outside the presets or larger projects, the customer clicks to chat and negotiate directly with a specific technician. The technician can then issue a custom quotation with either a single lump-sum or milestone-based payment terms.
* **Escrow Payment via Bank Transfer & Slip Verification**
  * When an agreement is reached (Preset or Custom), the customer pays securely via bank transfer and uploads their payment slip. 
  * The platform's administration verifies the slip and holds the money securely in an escrow system, assuring the technician that funds are secured before starting work.
* **Secure In-App Chat**
  Private chat channels open inside the platform to completely hide private phone numbers and email addresses:
  * **Customer <> Technician Chat** - Enables scope alignment, price/milestone negotiation, arrival scheduling, and issue photo sharing directly within the order scope.
  * **Customer <> Admin Chat** - Opens a direct line for customer support, payment/slip verification assistance, and filing service complaints.
  * **Technician <> Admin Chat** - Provides support for technicians regarding profile verification, payouts, and platform dispute resolutions.
* **Approve and Payout**
  * **Pay-per-Job** - For preset or simple jobs, the customer reviews the finished work and clicks to approve, releasing the full escrowed money to the technician all at once.
  * **Milestone Payments** - For big projects like full-house rewiring, the work is split into steps. The customer reviews and approves each step individually to release the escrowed money progressively.
* **Admin Dispatch & Management Dashboard**
  * Administrators can manage the service catalog, approve technician applications, track active orders, verify uploaded payment slips, and step in as mediators to resolve platform disputes.
